The Property

A house built into
the cliff itself.

Dream Valley serves you all welfare elements in one place — authentic food, calm residence, a picturesque view, private gardens, and exotic nature.

Located in the heart of the Chouf, 33 km from Beirut, Dream Valley is a cliff house and restaurant overlooking an amazing valley and river, surrounded by nature — the perfect place to reconnect and take a break from the crowd of the city.

Every unit is finished with the same intention: natural stone, warm wood, and a balcony that faces straight into the valley below.

Where to Stay

Three ways to
have the valley to yourself.

Every stay is private and self-contained — choose the one that matches your group.

120 m² · Sleeps up to 6

The Cliffside House

The largest unit on the property: two master bedrooms with double beds, plus a small double bed and sofa bed. A large living room with a working fireplace, full kitchen, and a private balcony that looks straight down the valley.

2 Master Bedrooms Fireplace Full Kitchen Valley Balcony
Private Pool & Garden

The Pool Chalet

A cosier retreat: one bedroom with two single beds, plus a living room with a 160cm double bed and kitchenette. Steps outside, a private garden wraps around your own plunge pool.

Entire Guesthouse · Sleeps 2

The Guesthouse

The simplest way to stay — one room with a double bed, a kitchenette, a bathroom, and a balcony with the same amazing valley view the whole property is known for. Ideal for couples.
